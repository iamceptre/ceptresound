<h2>Budget Repairs Trailer (with a Disco Polo tune!)</h2>

<p>For my <b>Majster Symulator</b> sound design test, I decided to add a little bonus giving it an <b>authentic Disco Polo soundtrack</b>. And it was way more challenging (and fun) that I though.</p>
<p>The extreme chessyness was the hardests thing to achieve, when even one too serious interval broke the vibe.</p>

<h3>Music Breakdown</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Yamaha DX7 <a href="https://github.com/asb2m10/dexed">(Dexed)</a></b> for the synth/tom sounds</li>
  <li>Classic <b>Drum Machine</b> beats</li>
  <li><b>Cheesy</b> melodies</li>
  <li><b>Simplistic</b> basslines, <i>also from Yamaha DX7</i></li>
</ul>
